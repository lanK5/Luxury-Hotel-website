INTRODUCTION

Luxury hotel is a local business that started out as a motel in 2005. At the time it only had 10 rooms and 1 branch in the countryside. Gradually it started growing and in order to accommodate more guests they had to build a bigger structure and thus came luxury hotels & resorts located at Upper Hill Nairobi county. Recently hotels have been closed, opened then closed again due to the pandemic. This poses a great challenge for those businesses without any automated services because they have to close and literally go out of business incurring great losses. Since the introduction of integrated powerful automated tools in today’s leading business organizations there has been a substantial increase in the level of business efficiency.This new system will allow for booking of seats, room reservations, ordering of meals and drinks and also shopping from outside while till logged into the luxury hotel website. This means that a guest can do online shopping for items that are not offered by the hotel but they’ll come from other shops using the hotels website because it will be linked to other businesses. Digitalization of their system will also make them more secure and available.
